About Me

Passionate About Healthcare Innovation

I am an AI Engineer specializing in healthcare applications with expertise in developing machine learning models for medical diagnosis. My work focuses on creating accurate, reliable, and accessible diagnostic tools for conditions like diabetes, pneumonia, tuberculosis, and malaria.

As the founder of DAS medhub, I lead a healthtech startup dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and everyday healthcare needs. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to all through innovative technology solutions.

In my role as an Extern Ambassador, I collaborate with healthcare institutions, research organizations, and technology partners to foster innovation and drive meaningful change in the healthcare sector.
