About Me

Healthcare AI Specialist

I am an AI Engineer with a passion for healthcare innovation, specializing in developing machine learning models for medical diagnosis. My expertise lies in creating accurate, reliable, and accessible diagnostic tools for conditions including diabetes, pneumonia, tuberculosis, and malaria.

As the founder of DAS medhub, I lead a healthtech startup dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and practical healthcare needs. Our mission is to democratize access to quality healthcare through innovative technological solutions.
